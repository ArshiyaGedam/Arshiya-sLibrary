The Palace of Illusions

6 star read for me !!

This book will always have a special place in my heart, the book not only opened a new world to me but a new sense of appreciation for the culture I am proudly a part of. After finishing the book I was ready to start it again and annotate it to show how much love I have for this book.

Thankyou Chitra Banerjee Divakaruni ma'am for bringing Panchali to life in her true yet beautiful form for all of us.
